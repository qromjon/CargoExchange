# CargoExchange Global Ltd Overview
CargoExchange Global Ltd is dedicated to revolutionizing the logistics and cargo shipping industry through innovative solutions and seamless connections. With a focus on efficiency, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we aim to provide unparalleled service to our clients.

## Non-Negotiable Rules
1. **Safety First**: All operations must prioritize the safety of personnel and cargo.
2. **Integrity**: We uphold transparency and honesty in all transactions.
3. **Timeliness**: Deliverables must be on time, every time.
4. **Respect**: All interactions should be respectful and professional.
5. **Compliance**: Adhere to all regulatory requirements and industry standards.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js, HTML5, CSS3
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Deployment**: Docker, AWS
